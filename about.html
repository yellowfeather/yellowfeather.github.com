About Yellow Feather

Established in 1997, Yellow Feather has been providing web and mobile software development to a wide range of clients across the industrial, commercial and public sectors for nearly 20 years.

At Yellow Feather we design, develop and support web and mobile applications for small and medium sized businesses. We help business to make more money by streamlining their operations, mobilising their workforce and automating their processes. We make technology work for you, so you have more time to focus on your business.

In short, Yellow Feather:

  • is small, professional and agile, when you speak to us you speak to the people working on your projects
  • works closely with our clients, you get exactly what you want
  • can speak your language, no techno mumbo jumbo!
  • offers great customer service and support
  • has experience across a broad range of technologies, we recommend or use the technology appropriate for your project
  • follows software development best practices such as object oriented design, test driven development, source control
